Privacy Policy for Liahona Linguist

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Liahona Linguist ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection & Processing

We do not collect personal information like your name, address, or phone number. However, to provide premium features, we collect the following anonymous data:

  • Purchase History: To manage your subscription status.
  • Device Identifiers: To link your subscription to your device.

This data is processed by RevenueCat. You can view their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

3. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by the respective app stores (Apple App Store or Google Play Store). We do not process or store your payment details directly. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's app store settings.
